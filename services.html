Services

Here is a list of services we can provide, customly catered to your different needs.

Transportation

Uber and Lyft

  • If you need assistance with transportation, we can work with Uber and Lyft to get you discounts and grants on a need-basis. Getting from point-A to point-B shouldn't be a hassal, or a hussle.

Other

Home Services

  • Our volunteers are equipped to make your life easier, from washing dishes all the way to hanging shelves. Connect with volunteers today by taking our survey.

Handicap Utilities

  • We work with brands like Walmart, Dick's Sporting Goods, and Amazon to bring you discounted and even free neccesisties to your lifestyle, such as wheelchairs, walkers, crutches, and more.

For a more extensive, detailed and personalized list of what is available, please take our survey. We'll use the information you provide to connect you with lifestyle aids that make sense for you.
